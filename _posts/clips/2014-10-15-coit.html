--- title: Anna Coit, North Stonington's grande dame, dies at age 106 layout: clip link: http://www.theday.com/article/20141015/NWS01/141019863/ source: The Day tags: - Profiles - Features ---

Anna North Coit, prolific poet, founding member of the North Stonington Historical Society and a role model to friends of many ages, died Wednesday morning at Apple Rehab in Mystic. She was 106.
