Peter J. Schultz visits the transfer station every few weeks to dispose of his garbage. To him, the trip is more than just a chore. As he dropped off some items on Sunday, he declared the transfer station the town's "social center."

On second thought, he said, the dump might have competition from the Polish Citizens Club, which he visits mostly for funeral receptions.

"You go to the Polish club to die, and you go here to live," he said of the transfer station.
